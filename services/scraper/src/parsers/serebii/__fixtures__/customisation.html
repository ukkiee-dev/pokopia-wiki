Ditto Customisation

A feature, first introduced within Pokémon Battle Revolution, returns once again . As before, you have the ability to customise your character's appearance in the game.

Ditto Selection

When you start the game, like recent Pokémon games, you will start off by selecting your base Ditto between the two options. From there, you have the ability to change the look, hair and skin colour of your Ditto. This can be done at any time whenever you go to a large mirror no matter where it is placed.
Outfits

As the game takes place in the Kanto region long after a catastrophe hits, there are few shops for you to go to. Instead, Ditto learns various outfits from looking at magazines talking about various trainers from around the world

Some of these magazines can be found in the locations in Kanto while many are randomly found during excursions to Dream Islands
