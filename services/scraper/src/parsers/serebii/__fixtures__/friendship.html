Friendship

As with all Pokïŋ―mon games, Pokïŋ―mon Pokopia has a friendship system with the Pokïŋ―mon in the game. This is different to the Comfort Level that they have, though their friendship can affect the growth of that as well.

Getting a Pokïŋ―mon happy is fairly simple. If they come to you with a request, if you complete it their friendship gauge will increase. If you give them a gift, their friendship level will also increase.

As a Pokïŋ―mon's friendship level with you increases, they will start to be happy seeing you around, and will even sometimes give you gifts of various materials they have found. As it approaches the highest level, the Pokïŋ―mon will start to call you by your given name rather than Ditto.

Games

Periodically, a Pokïŋ―mon will come to you asking if you'd like to play a game. This can be a quiz, typically about another Pokïŋ―mon that the Pokïŋ―mon is friends with, or it can be a game like Look This Way

Look This Way has you have to pick what way the opponent is going to look. If you get it right, you win a point. If you get it wrong, the Pokïŋ―mon wins the point. The most points after three rounds wins

With the game, you will always get a friendship boost for playing, but if you win the friendship will increase even more.

Best Friends

Once you have reached maximum friendship, the Pokïŋ―mon will come to you and announce that it is your best friend. Now it will always be happy to see you and, in addition to that, it will give a special mark on the Pokïŋ―mon's Pokïŋ―dex entry indicating that it is a Best Friend.
