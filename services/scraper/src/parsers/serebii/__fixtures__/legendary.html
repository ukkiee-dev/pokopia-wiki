Legendary Pok’┐Įmon

As with many Pok’┐Įmon games, Legendary Pok’┐Įmon can be found throughout the game. In Pok’┐Įmon Pokopia, they act much like other Pok’┐Įmon with their own specialties, desires and so forth but there are a few changes

Legendary and Mythical Pok’┐Įmon have a greater effect on the Environment level of the area you bring them to. As you improve their comfort level, it becomes easier to raise the entire environment level of the area

Dream Islands

Several of the Legendary Pok’┐Įmon can be obtained by going through various Dream Islands. If you go to some Dream Islands, you will have a chance of getting the Legendary Beasts and Mewtwo. You can fine tune the islands you get using certain dolls to start your journey but it isn't a guarantee.

Eevee Doll: Suicune
Pikachu Doll: Raikou
Arcanine Doll: Entei
Dragonite Doll: Mewtwo

Once you have spoken to the Pok’┐Įmon, you will be able to bring them back to the region and set them up in a home.

Ho-Oh & Lugia

Throughout your gameplay, you will be seeing Ho-Oh and Lugia flying overhead. If you wait for them to leave, they will drop off a Rainbow Feather or Silver Feather respectively.

After you have befriended Articuno, Zapdos and Moltres, you will get a recipe for a Tidal Bell which requires 5 Rare Pok’┐ĮMetal and 5 Silver Feather to make, and if you befriend Raikou, Entei & Suicune you'll get the recipe for the Clear Bell which requires 5 Rare Pok’┐ĮMetal and 5 Rainbow Feather to make.

With these items in hand, when Ho-Oh or Lugia fly overhead, you can place tohose items and ring the bell to get them to come down to talk and befriend.

Volcanion

After you have finished Rocky Ridges, you will be able to befriend Volcanion. To get it to reappear so it can move in, you will need to set up a second party in Rocky Ridges which involves getting the mood up to 100 again. Then, when the party is finished, load up a cannon and some fireworks and Volcanion will appear again and ask to join as the parties always seem fun. From here, it will now live in Rocky Ridges.

Mew

Mew is a bit different in how it is found. Unlike the random ones found in the Dream Island from the get-go, or the ones that you find in the story before finding them in Dream Island, here you need to find 27 different Mysterious Slates. These can be found randomly in shiny patches of ground. Then, take then to the ruins near the Pok’┐Įmon Center in the Withered Wastelands. Put the blocks on the corresponding tile to make a Mew picture and Mew will then be available to find.
