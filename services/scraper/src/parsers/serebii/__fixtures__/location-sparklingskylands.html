Sparkling Skylands

The Sparkling Skylands are a giant area that focused around what used to be Celadon and Saffron Cities. The catastrophe caused the ground to shoot up into the sky.

With the Pokémon you find here, your task is to rebuild Silph Co. into a new building to bring back humans and Pokémon
