Crafting & Recipes

Although you will find many items throughout the region, and you can trade for some, and purchase for others with daily specials on what you can buy, a big way to get the items you need is to craft them. However, in order to do so, you need to have the recipe.

Recipes can be found in a myriad of ways; the PC Shop has a batch with more unlocking as you increase your environment level in each area, others are founda s part of the story and the rest are found randomly in ripples of water which reset each day.

Once you have the recipe, you can go to any work bench and, if you have the required materials, you can build the item and you can even build multiple of them at the same time if you have the materials.
