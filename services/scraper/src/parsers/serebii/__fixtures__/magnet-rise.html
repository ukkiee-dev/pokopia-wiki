Magnet Rise

After finishing the story, you can find Magnemite hidden under the rocket that left the Withered Wastelands. This Magnemite will teach you the new transformation, Magnet Rise. This transformation is one of the most important in the game as it has some great versatlitiy

With this, you have the ability to fly anywhere, adjust rotation etc., but it does use PP for each use

Building

One of the key elements of Magnet Rise is the building feature. While as your standard human form you can place one block at a time, in Magnet Rise Ditto can place up to 9 blocks in one go

With this, you can rotate it to be vertical and horizontal, and you can also set it to replace blocks so if there's 9 Cliff Rocks in front of you, but you wish to replace them with Field Grass, you can select the Replace mode and will switch the blocks over, and put the Cliff Rock in your back.

Magnet Rise

The other feature is the Break featuee. If you press Y by an item, it will break it up and give you the resource. If it's a plant or a hedge, it will give you the seeds and other items such as Worn lumber will become Lumber. This uses less PP in general than using Rock Smash or Cut to do it, and is a lot quicker

However, if you hold the button and charge it, it will take the entire item and place it in your bag. This means various materials such as Stone debris, Broken Streetlights etc. which can either be not picked up, or have to be broken down into different materials will be able to be placed in your bag. This also includes partially grown plants and more,
