Painting

Once you reach Bleak Beach, you will be able to meet with Smearguru. Smearguru has got the unique Specialty of Paint which allows for it to paint various items. Throughout the game, a number of the items you receive will be able to be customised using a variety of paint. Paint can be obtained by having a Pokémon with the Crush specialty crush berries, or by purchasing it in the shop

BerryCommonBonus
Leppa Berry Red Paint White Paint
Chesto Berry Blue Paint White Paint
Pecha Berry Pink Paint Black Paint
Rawst Berry Cyan Paint Black Paint
Aspear Berry Yellow Paint Black Paint
Lum Berry Green Paint White Paint

You can also buy paint balloons in the shop and craft them in order to be able to throw the paint at the items in question yourself.

Some items can also be painted in two different parts rather than just one allowing for you to mix and match with colours how you see fit.

There are 18 different colours you can paint things in.

Patterns

In addition to paint, some items will be able to have specific patterns painted on. While Smearguru will know some patterns from the beginning, you'll be able to find 22 more patterns as you travel through Dream Islands.

These patternpatterns require a larger amount of paint and will typically only go on the fabric areas of any given item.

In addition to finding them in the Dream Islands, if you exchange Honey with Vespiquen, you will get an item with a pattern including unique patterns exclusive to the items. You may also get items with patterns available in the in-game daily shop. Show Smearguru them and you will get access to them later. T
