Lost Relics

As you explore the various areas in the game, you will sometimes find blocks that are glowing. If you destroy these blocks, you have a chance of finding rare items liuke Mysterious Slabs, Fossils or the Lost Relics, though sometimes they may end up preparing to explode, requiring you to cool them first.

Lost Relics are items of old that you need to show to Professor Tangrowth to get him to Appraise it and when you do, you will get an item. These items are rare and typically exclusive to this, though you may find a few of them out and about.

Large Relics are generally larger items that can be displayed on their own, with several being required for various habitats. Small Relics will require being put in a frame if you wish to put them on display.

You will have the ability to 3D Print the Large Relics, but they will require 5 Rare PokéMetal Ingots to create.
