Furniture

As you decorate your house and the various islands and dwellings throughout the region, furniture is often requested. Some of the furniture has the Relaxation and Decoration flags which some Pokémon may request to increase their Comfy Level while others are mostly there for utility and to look good.

Furniture can frequently be found in the stores in the daily specials, or at vaarious Trade Outposts, as well as left around the ruins of the old Kanto cities for you to pick up. All the furniture can also have recipes found throughout the region such as in ripples and more. You can also 3D Print them all to get more.

Many of the furniture can also have their colourings changed by Smeargle, either once or in two different parts allowing for you to fully customise the items how you see fit.
